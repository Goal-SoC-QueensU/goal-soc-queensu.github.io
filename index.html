Dr. Mahzabeen Emu Receives Governor General’s Academic Gold Medal

Dr. Mahzabeen Emu, a recent PhD graduate from Queen’s University and alumna of the GOAL Lab, has been awarded the prestigious Governor General’s Academic Gold Medal. She has also joined Memorial University of Newfoundland as a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor.
